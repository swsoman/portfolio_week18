ABOUT ME

I am a User Experience and User Interface designer with an experience in Architectural design. I have been into designing since I was in high school. As a contemporary artist, I tend to like clean minimal design with little decorative elements. After spending a few years in the field as a Architect, I have developed a strong analytical sense towards designing, which has made me even more of a user centered designer. I like to work on tasks that challenge my design thinking abilities. Besides that, I enjoy working on projects that solve everyday problems and help improve my designing skills as well as expand my knowledge from time to time.

User Centered | Goal oriented
Critical Reasoning

My UX strategy is simple, analyzing where we are in the design process, listing down immediate and future goals and taking the steps towards the ultimate objective. I like to be realistic about task timelines and enjoy working in a fun, collaborative work environment.
